Welcome to The Reader's Cafe

Founded in 2014, The Reader's Café is a cozy retreat for book lovers, coffee enthusiasts, and foodies alike. Surrounded by over 1000 books, our wood-paneled space offers a peaceful escape where great reads meet comforting bites.

We serve handcrafted coffee, freshly brewed teas, and a menu full of delicious options — from pizzas and pastas to burgers and more. Every dish is prepared with care, offering generous portions and rich flavors that pair perfectly with a good book.

Our Story

The Reader's Café began as a dream between two college friends who wanted to create a space where people could enjoy great food, good books, and a warm atmosphere. In 2014, we opened our doors in Indirapuram, bringing this vision to life and offering a retreat for book lovers and foodies alike.

Each dish we serve is crafted with care — from the local ingredients we source to the way we prepare every meal with love and attention to detail.
